# Privacy Policy — BPL App

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

## 1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how BluePulse Logistics LLC ("BluePulse," "we," "our," or "us") handles information accessed through the **BPL App**, an internal application built and operated by BluePulse Logistics for use solely by BluePulse employees and authorized contractors.

The BPL App connects to QuickBooks Online (QBO) for the sole purpose of automating internal invoice itemization for BluePulse's own QBO company. The BPL App is **not a public application**, is **not offered to third parties**, and does **not connect to QuickBooks accounts of any company other than BluePulse Logistics LLC**.

## 2. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies only to authorized BluePulse employees and contractors who use the BPL App as part of their work duties. The BPL App is not available to the general public, customers, or any external party.

## 3. Information We Access

When authorized BluePulse personnel connect the BPL App to BluePulse's QuickBooks Online company, the application accesses the following data through Intuit's official QuickBooks Online API:

- Invoice records (invoice numbers, customers, amounts, line items)
- Item / product / service records (used for fee mapping)
- Class records (used for cost-center / department categorization)
- Limited customer reference data (names linked to invoices)

The BPL App does **not** access:
- Bank account or payment processing information
- Employee payroll data
- Tax filings or filings data
- Personal financial information of customers

## 4. How We Use This Information

The BPL App uses accessed QuickBooks data exclusively to:

1. Read existing draft invoices pushed by ShipHero (BluePulse's warehouse management system)
2. Replace generic line items with itemized fee categories
3. Maintain an internal audit trail of changes made to BluePulse's own invoices

All processing happens internally for BluePulse's accounting purposes only.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored in an encrypted local database accessible only to BluePulse's internal infrastructure.
- The BPL App is hosted on a private network (Microsoft Windows 365 cloud PC) and accessed only via authenticated, internal channels.
- No QuickBooks data is transmitted to third-party services, advertising networks, or external storage providers.
- Audit logs of invoice modifications are retained internally for accounting and compliance purposes.

## 6. Data Sharing

We **do not** share, sell, rent, or disclose QuickBooks data accessed by the BPL App with any third party. The data remains strictly within BluePulse Logistics' internal accounting workflow.

## 7. Data Retention

QuickBooks OAuth tokens remain stored only while the BPL App is actively connected to BluePulse's QuickBooks Online company. Disconnecting the app revokes these tokens.

Audit logs of invoice modifications are retained per BluePulse's standard accounting record-keeping policies, consistent with applicable U.S. tax and business record retention requirements.

## 8. User Rights and Disconnection

Authorized BluePulse personnel may disconnect the BPL App from QuickBooks Online at any time through the QuickBooks Online "Apps" management interface or through the BPL App's own disconnect functionality. Upon disconnection, all OAuth tokens are revoked.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our internal practices or applicable legal requirements. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above.
